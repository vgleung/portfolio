About

web and graphic designer

Vivian Leung

I strive to create meaningful interfaces since design is visual communication. I started my interest in design initially but after a few courses and one year of internships later, I found myself more interested in websites in particular.

Over the years, instead of building interfaces, I now find myself focusing more on creating interactive systems. Through understanding human computer interactions I am able to create minimalistic systems to help users navigate effortlessly.
